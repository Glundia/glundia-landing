Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and phone number (for account verification)
  • Profile Photos: Optional profile pictures (stored securely in encrypted storage)
  • Location Data: Your approximate location (city/region) to show you drops near you and help you find local causes to support. We never share your precise location.
  • Drop Activity: Tickets purchased, drops created to raise funds for causes, and prizes won
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store your full credit card details
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app usage analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide drop services to help people raise funds for medical needs, pet rescue, urgent expenses, and community causes
  • Show you drops near your location that you can support
  • Process payments and ticket purchases
  • Send transactional notifications (drop results, prize wins, account updates)
  • Prevent fraud and ensure fair play
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We share your data with:

  • Payment Processors: To process secure transactions (we use industry-standard payment providers)
  • Analytics Services: Firebase Analytics for app performance monitoring (anonymized data)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our legal rights

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Correction: Update or correct any incorrect information
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails (transactional emails remain active)
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward to comply with legal obligations. After you request account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Transaction records may be retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS encryption
  • Passwords are hashed and salted
  • Database encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting employee data access

7. Children's Privacy

Glundia is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at hi@glundia.com.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze app usage (via Firebase Analytics)

You can manage cookie preferences in your device settings, though this may affect app functionality.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests, or concerns, please contact us:

  • Email: hi@glundia.com
  • Response time: 5-7 business days

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notification to your registered address
  • In-app notification
  • Notice on this page with updated "Last updated" date

Continued use of Glundia after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the EU/EEA, we process your data based on:

  • Contract Performance: To provide drop services you've signed up for
  • Legitimate Interests: To improve our services, prevent fraud, and ensure security
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Consent: For marketing communications (you can withdraw consent anytime)
