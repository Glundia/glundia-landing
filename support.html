Frequently Asked Questions

Important Platform Disclaimer

  • Glundia acts as a contact point between drop organizers and participants. We facilitate connections but do not organize or oversee the drops themselves.
  • Glundia never holds money from organizers or buyers. Glundia's fees are processed through secure in-app purchases. The ticket payments between buyers and organizers are handled independently outside the app after the initial contact through Glundia.
  • While we facilitate the initial contact between participants and organizers, Glundia is not responsible for any incomplete transactions. Participants and organizers complete their ticket payments independently outside the app after connecting through Glundia.
  • Drop organizers are solely responsible for prize delivery, refund policies, and their drop's legitimacy.

How do I support a cause?

Browse available drops on the home screen, tap on a drop to view the cause details, select your desired ticket numbers, and connect with the drop creator through the app. Glundia facilitates the initial contact between you and the creator, and you'll complete the ticket payment directly with the organizer outside the app. Your contribution helps fund the creator's stated cause.

When are drop winners announced?

Each drop has a scheduled draw date and time displayed on the drop details page. Winners are selected using cryptographically secure random number generation and are notified immediately via email and in-app notification once the draw is completed.

How do I claim my prize?

When you win a drop:

  • You'll receive an email and in-app notification
  • Go to the "My Prizes" section in your profile
  • Follow the instructions provided by the drop creator
  • You typically have 30 days to claim your prize
  • Contact the creator directly if you need assistance

Can I get a refund on ticket purchases?

Ticket purchases are contributions to support causes. Since payments are made directly between participants and drop organizers outside the Glundia app, refund policies are set by individual drop creators. Please review drop details and the creator's refund policy carefully before purchasing. For refund requests, contact the drop organizer directly through the app.

Glundia only processes fees for platform usage (via in-app purchases). We do not hold or control ticket payments between participants and organizers.

How do I create a drop for my cause?

  1. Tap the "Create Drop" button in the app
  2. Describe your cause or need (medical, pet rescue, urgent expense, community project, etc.)
  3. Upload photos related to your cause (optional but recommended)
  4. Set ticket price, quantity, and maximum tickets per user
  5. Choose a draw date (at least 24 hours in the future)
  6. Review and publish your drop
  7. Manage your drop from the "My Drops" section

What causes can I raise funds for?

Glundia supports fundraising for legitimate needs and causes, including:

  • Medical treatments, surgeries, and healthcare expenses
  • Pet shelters, veterinary care, and animal rescue
  • Urgent personal expenses (rent, utilities, emergency repairs)
  • Community projects and mutual aid initiatives
  • Educational expenses and skill development

See our Terms of Service for prohibited causes (fraudulent claims, illegal activities, pure gambling without genuine need).

How are funds distributed to creators?

Glundia acts as a contact point between drop organizers and participants. We facilitate connections but do not handle ticket payments. After you connect with a drop creator through Glundia:

  • You complete ticket payments directly with the organizer outside the app
  • Glundia never holds money from organizers or buyers
  • Glundia's fees are processed separately through secure in-app purchases
  • Creators should use funds for their stated cause/need

How do I verify a cause is legitimate?

Before supporting a drop:

  • Read the cause description carefully
  • Check the creator's profile and history of drops
  • Look for supporting documentation or photos
  • Trust your instincts - if something seems suspicious, report it
  • Review comments from other participants

Remember: Glundia reviews causes but cannot guarantee legitimacy. Support causes you believe in.

What if I suspect a fraudulent cause?

If you encounter suspicious drops, fraudulent creators, or inappropriate content:

  • Report immediately to hi@glundia.com
  • Include the drop ID or creator username
  • Provide screenshots if possible
  • We investigate all reports within 24-48 hours
  • Fraudulent creators face account termination and fund withholding

How are drop winners selected?

Winners are selected using cryptographically secure random number generation, ensuring fair and unbiased draws. The process is:

  • Completely random (no human intervention)
  • Transparent and auditable
  • Conducted automatically at the scheduled draw time
  • Cannot be manipulated by creators or participants

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account:

  1. Open the Glundia app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Scroll to Account section
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, email hi@glundia.com with your deletion request. Your data will be removed within 30 days as described in our Privacy Policy.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! All payments are processed through our secure, PCI-compliant payment provider. We never store your full credit card details on our servers. Your financial information is encrypted and protected using industry-standard security measures.

What happens if I don't respond to a prize win notification?

If you don't respond to a prize claim notification within 30 days:

  • The prize may be forfeited
  • The drop creator may select an alternate winner
  • You will not be eligible for a refund

Make sure to enable notifications and check your email regularly!

Can I cancel a drop I created?

Yes, but with restrictions:

  • Before any tickets are sold: Cancel anytime with no penalty
  • After tickets are sold (less than 50%): Contact support at hi@glundia.com
  • After 50% of tickets are sold: Cancellation is not allowed (must proceed with draw)

If you cancel, all ticket purchasers receive automatic refunds.

Common Technical Problems

App crashes or freezes

  • Make sure you're running the latest app version (check App Store/Play Store)
  • Restart your device
  • Clear app cache (Settings → Apps → Glundia → Clear Cache)
  • Reinstall the app if problem persists

Payment failed or declined

  • Check that your card has sufficient funds
  • Verify card details are entered correctly
  • Try a different payment method
  • Contact your bank to ensure they're not blocking the transaction

Can't log in to my account

  • Verify you're using the correct email address
  • Try "Forgot Password" to reset your password
  • Check your spam folder for password reset emails
  • If using social login (Google/Apple), ensure you're signed in to that account

Not receiving notifications

  • Check notification settings in the Glundia app (Profile → Settings → Notifications)
  • Verify notifications are enabled in your device settings (Settings → Notifications → Glundia)
  • Check your email spam folder
  • Ensure your email address is verified in your profile
