3. Platform Purpose & Drop Guidelines

3.1 Community Fundraising

Glundia is a community platform designed to help people raise funds for legitimate needs and causes, including:

  • Medical treatments, surgeries, and healthcare expenses
  • Pet shelters, veterinary care, and animal rescue operations
  • Urgent personal expenses (rent, utilities, emergency repairs)
  • Community projects and mutual aid initiatives
  • Educational expenses and skill development

3.2 How Drops Work

  • Drop creators describe their cause or need and set a fundraising goal
  • Participants purchase tickets to support the cause
  • All drop draws are conducted using cryptographically secure random number generation
  • One winner is selected randomly when the drop closes
  • Funds raised (minus platform fees) go to the drop creator to address their stated need
  • Each ticket has an equal probability of winning (fair and transparent)

3.3 Creator Responsibilities

3.4 Participant Responsibilities

  • Understand that ticket purchases are final and support the cause regardless of winning
  • Respond to prize claim notifications within 30 days if you win
